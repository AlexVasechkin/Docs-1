allow\_forward\_across\_contexts
--------------------------------

**Name**: Allow Forwarding Across Contexts   
**Type**: Yes/No   
**Default**: No   
**Available In:** 2.1+

When enabled, Symlinks and modX::sendForward() API calls can forward requests to Resources in other Contexts.
