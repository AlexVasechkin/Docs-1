What is DefaultUserGroup?
-------------------------

Plugin assigns new users to one or more User Groups, optionally with specified role(s).

Package Information
-------------------

- License: GPLv2
- Requires: Revolution 2.0.x or greater
- Supports: mysql,sqlsrv
