## cache\_noncore\_lexicon\_topics

**Name**: Cache Non-Core Lexicon Topics
**Type**: Yes/No
**Default**: Yes

When disabled, non-core Lexicon Topics will be not be cached. This is useful to disable when developing your own Extras.
