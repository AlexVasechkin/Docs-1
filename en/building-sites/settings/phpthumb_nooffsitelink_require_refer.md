## phpthumb\_nooffsitelink\_require\_refer

**Name**: phpThumb Offsite Linking Require Referrer
**Type**: Yes/No
**Default**: No
**Available In:** 2.0.8+

If enabled, any offsite linking attempts will be rejected without a valid referrer header.
