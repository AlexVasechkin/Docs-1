## friendly\_alias\_lowercase\_only

**Name**: FURL Lowercase Aliases 
**Type**: Yes/No 
**Default**: Yes 
**Available In**: Revolution 2.0.8+

Determines whether to allow only lowercase characters in a Resource alias.
