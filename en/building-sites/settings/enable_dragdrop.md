## enable\_dragdrop

**Name**: Enable Drag/Drop in Resource/Element Trees
**Type**: Yes/No
**Default**: Yes
**Available In**: Revolution 2.0.8+

If off, will prevent dragging and dropping in Resource and Element trees.
