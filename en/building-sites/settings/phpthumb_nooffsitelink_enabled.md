## phpthumb\_nooffsitelink\_enabled

**Name**: phpThumb Offsite Linking Disabled
**Type**: Yes/No
**Default**: No
**Available In:** 2.0.8+

Disables the ability for others to use phpThumb to render images on their own sites.
