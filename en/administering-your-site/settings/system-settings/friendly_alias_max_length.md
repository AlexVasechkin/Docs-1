friendly\_alias\_max\_length
----------------------------

**Name**: AFURL Alias Maximum Length   
**Type**: Textfield   
**Default**: 0   
**Available In**: Revolution 2.0.8+

If greater than zero, the maximum number of characters to allow in a Resource alias. Zero equals unlimited.
