## phpthumb\_nooffsitelink\_valid\_domains

**Name**: phpThumb Offsite Linking Valid Domains 
**Type**: Textfield 
**Default**: {http\_host} 
**Available In:** 2.0.8+

A comma-delimited list of hostnames that are valid referrers for offsite linking.
