phpthumb\_nohotlink\_enabled
----------------------------

**Name**: phpThumb Hotlinking Disabled   
**Type**: combo-boolean   
**Default**: Yes/No   
**Available In:** 2.0.8+

Remote servers are allowed in the src parameter unless you disable hotlinking in phpThumb.
